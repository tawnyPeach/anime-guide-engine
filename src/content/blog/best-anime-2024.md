## Best Anime of 2024

2024 has been an incredible year for anime fans. From long-awaited sequels to fresh new originals, the industry delivered some truly memorable experiences. Here are our top picks for the best anime of the year.

### Solo Leveling

The adaptation fans waited years for finally arrived, and it did not disappoint. Solo Leveling brought Sung Jin-Woo's journey from the weakest hunter to an unstoppable force with stunning animation by A-1 Pictures.

**Why watch:** Incredible action sequences, satisfying power progression, and a protagonist you cannot help but root for.

### Frieren: Beyond Journey's End (Continued)

Frieren continued its exploration of time, memory, and what it means to connect with others. The show's deliberate pacing and beautiful storytelling made every episode feel meaningful.

**Why watch:** Thoughtful storytelling that rewards patient viewers with profound emotional moments.

### Dandadan

One of the most creative and entertaining new series of the year. Dandadan blends supernatural horror, sci-fi aliens, and high school comedy into something truly unique.

**Why watch:** Wild creativity, fantastic animation, and a perfect balance of humor and heart.

### Oshi no Ko Season 2

The entertainment industry drama continued with even higher stakes. Season 2 dove deeper into the dark side of fame while maintaining its thriller-like tension.

**Why watch:** Sharp commentary on the entertainment industry wrapped in compelling mystery.
